BEST OPTION: Use Email Sign-Up (Most Reliable)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

NETLIFY (Recommended):
1. Go to: https://www.netlify.com
2. Click "Sign up"
3. Click "Sign up with Email" (NOT Google/GitHub)
4. Enter your email
5. Create password
6. Verify email (check inbox)
7. Log in
8. Drag your game1 folder onto the page
9. DONE!

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

ALTERNATIVE: Vercel (Also Easy)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Go to: https://vercel.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Use EMAIL (not OAuth)
4. Verify email
5. Click "Add New" → "Project"
6. Drag your game1 folder
7. Click "Deploy"
8. DONE!

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHY EMAIL WORKS BETTER:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- No OAuth errors
- More reliable
- Works every time
